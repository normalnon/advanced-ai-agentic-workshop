50 AI skills. One repo. Zero hiring.

I spent the last 2 weeks adding a social intelligence layer to our open-source affiliate marketing skills.

The problem most AI-written affiliate content has:

Written from training data. No engagement signals. No real sources. Just generic AI output that sounds like every other review on the internet.

Here's what changed in v1.2:

→ trending-content-scout scans YouTube, TikTok, X, and Reddit. Returns top content sorted by engagement. Shows which formats dominate, which hooks work, and where the gaps are.

→ content-angle-ranker scores 8-12 angles on 4 dimensions: platform fit, competition level, engagement prediction, creator fit.

→ content-research-brief collects 5-10 real source articles. Auto-tags them (AI, Funding, Tools, Trends). Extracts stats and quotes. Your content has real data — not hallucinated numbers.

→ infographic-generator creates branded visuals for every post. LinkedIn-optimized 1080×1350. Self-contained HTML or structured spec.

→ traffic-analyzer evaluates advertiser website health before you commit to promoting.

The flow:
Scout → Rank → Research → Write → Visualize → Track → Loop

50 skills across 8 stages. All APIs optional — works with web_search alone. MIT license.

Works with Claude Code, Pi, ChatGPT, Gemini, Cursor, or any AI that reads text.
